Care Guide

Care Instructions

Proper care ensures your fashion items stay beautiful and last longer. Follow these tips for best results.

Wig & Hair Care

  • • Gently detangle with a wide-tooth comb
  • • Wash with mild shampoo in cool water
  • • Air dry on a wig stand
  • • Store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight
  • • Avoid excessive heat styling

Footwear Care

  • • Clean with appropriate cleaner for material
  • • Stuff with paper to maintain shape
  • • Store in a dust bag or box
  • • Rotate wear to extend lifespan
  • • Apply protective spray for weather resistance

Clothing Care

  • • Check care labels before washing
  • • Wash similar colors together
  • • Use gentle cycle for delicate items
  • • Hang or fold immediately after drying
  • • Iron on appropriate temperature settings

Jewelry Care

  • • Clean with soft cloth after each wear
  • • Store separately to avoid scratches
  • • Remove before bathing or swimming
  • • Keep away from perfumes and chemicals
  • • Polish regularly to maintain shine
